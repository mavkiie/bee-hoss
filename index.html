Introduction

Save the Bees, Savor the Flavor

Bee Hoss is an artisan honey hot sauce that delivers a unique blend of sweet-heat flavors while giving back to its community. By harvesting real honey from the bees we help protect and sourcing from local farms we ensure a guarantee of good flavor and good community.

Our Story

Alex Crespo brought Bee Hoss to life in 2013. He started out bottling up the honey hot sauce as gifts for family and friends before realizing he wanted to take his creation to the next level. The story is simple. Alex wanted to create a more flavorful food experience while also helping community businesses and bee conservation. Getting a start right in the heart of Baltimore, Maryland, it was only natural for him to repay the local community that made this dream possible.

Moon Valley Farm grows specialty and staple vegetables and herbs on 70 acres in Woodsboro, Maryland for a year-round Community-Supported Agriculture program and local businesses.

Roots Farm Habanero / Jalapeno / Cayenne

Uniting our community through the cultivation of the earth and stewardship of the land. By extending the benefits we enjoy to others, we challenge ourselves to be aware of our environmental and social impact and create a living classroom to stimulate growth and learning.
